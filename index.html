From perplexed to expert

The crime writing workshop in Stockholm inspired me to start my own novel

I have read many crime fiction books based in Scandinavia, and always dreamed of writing my own novel. We learnt techniques to generate ideas, plan a story, for editing and the writing itself. The Nordic setting helped me come up with a concept of my own, which I began writing in Stockholm. I am taking the Oslo and Copenhagen tours for sequels in Norway and Denmark!
